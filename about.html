About

I am a multidisciplinary designer, maker, multimedia producer, and researcher exploring the intersection between humanity and technology.

My research explores how non-human intelligences might meaningfully integrate into daily life, from supporting neurodivergent children to reimagining conventions of labor, identity, and care. I imagine potential futures where robots are emotionally resonant with their human counterparts.

For Hire: I'm currently doing the freelancing thing. Reach out if you have need of creative technology, human-centered design, multimedia production, and more. I'd love to hear about what you're working on.
