La Rosa Collective

Community Art
2019
Branding and Activation

Over the years, I've collaborated with Little Rock creative collective, La Rosa Collective and founder Maxi Dominguez, to produce multimedia for communications, screenprinting assets, one-off and collaborations with local brands.

My work with LRC has revolved around analog print, using the nuances and textures of print in my digital work and designing with constraints for economical screenprint production. I also acted as a general consulting, giving feedback and workshopping as needed.
