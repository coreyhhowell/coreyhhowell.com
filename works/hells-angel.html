About

Developed complete visual identity for Kenney's debut album Hell's Angel, drawing inspiration from 1990s Memphis underground hip-hop aesthetics (Tommy Wright III, Three 6 Mafia, DJ Squeaky, DJ Zirk). Designed primary album cover, back cover, and three single covers for Devil in My Ear, Forza, and Texas. Extended design language to bootleg-style merchandise and promotional materials for November 2022 listening party.

Created experimental visuals using custom cathode-ray tube feedback patterns as displacement maps. Applied heavy distortion effects to shot footage and finished with custom LUT developed in IWLTBAP.
